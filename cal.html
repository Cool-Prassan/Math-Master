What you need to know for grade 1:


1. Count and identify numbers

2. Subtraction and addition for 1-digit-numbers

3. Understand quantity (more & less)

4. Familiarity with patterns and shapes

5. Knowledge of place values (ones, tens etc.)



What you need to know for grade 2:


1. Addition and subtraction with one and two-digit-numbers up to 100

2. Knowledge of odd and even numbers

3. Use number charts

4. Identify number patterns up to 100

5. Knowledge of place values (ones, tens, hundreds etc.)



What you need to know for grade 3:


1. Addition, subtraction. division, multiplication of numbers

2. Knowledge of fact families

3. Read and write large numbers (hundreds, thousands etc.)

4. Begin to read time

5. Begin to learn the value of money (dimes, nickels, dollars etc.)



What you need to know for grade 4:


1. Addition, subtraction. division, multiplication of numbers

2. Knowledge of fractions (equivilent, ordering etc.)

3. Begin to solve word problems

4. Knwledge of factors to 100

5. Compare decimals



What you need to know for grade 5:


1. Addition, subtraction. division, multiplication of numbers, fractions, decimals etc.

2. Identify rational numbers

3. Identify integers on number lines

4. Understand math in diffrent formats (numbers, drawings, charts etc.)

5. Knowledge to use models to solve problems



What you need to know for grade 6:


1. Knowledge of number sense and operations

2. Knowledge of geometry, measurement, algebra

3. Begin to understand functions and probability

4. Fluently divide, multiply, add and subtract with multi-digit-numbers

5. Fluently divide, multiply, add and subtract with decimals



What you need to know for grade 7:


1. Knowledge to preform most operation with whole numbers and decimals

2. Begin to represent percentages

3. Intepret and compute rational numbers

4. Work with negative and positve number on a coordinate plane

5. Understand graphs and charts to solve problems



What you need to know for grade 8:


1. Begin to understand algebraic and geometric concepts

2. Deeper understanding in measurements and probability

3. Knowledge of square roots and powers

4. Comparing and solving questions with functions

5. Understand linear equations and functions



What you need to know for grade 9:


1. Begin understanding circle geometry

2. Dividing polynomials

3. Deeper understanding in surface area, exponents and rational numbers

4. Knowledge of algbra ||, pre-calculus, trigonometry

5. Focus on linear equations



What you need to know for grade 10:


1. Connections with math and reality

2. Develope reasoning and problem solving

3. Deeper understanding in geometry and algebra |

4. Knowledge/focusing on algebra ||

5. Deeper understanding in calculus



What you need to know for grade 11:


1. Understanding grade 11 functions (rational expressions, trigonometrig expressions etc.)

2. Deeper understanding in statistics

3. Deeper understanding in periodic and quadratic functions

4. Graphing exponential and logarithmic functions

5. Focusing on IMO



What you need to know for grade 12:


1. Understand advanced functions

2. Deeper understanding in algebra ||

3. Demonstrate solutions with functions

4. Deep understanding in varietys to represent solutions

5. Developing solutions with the taught lessons in a variety of ways
